Haoxiang Sun received his Bachelor degree in Biochemistry-Molecular Biology from UCSB in 2021, and Master's degree in Bioengineering from UCSD in 2023. During 2023-2024, he worked as an RA at Harvard Medical School on developing microcapsule platform for multi-step single-cell analysis. In 2025, Haoxiang joined the Wang lab, with a focus on sequencing pipeline development for more effective & scalable methylation sequencing.
